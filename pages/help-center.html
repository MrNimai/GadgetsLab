Help Center

Find answers to common questions and issues.

Why is my cart empty?

Your cart might be empty if you have cleared your browser's cache or cookies, as the cart is stored locally on your browser. Please ensure you are not using a private or incognito window, which does not save your session data.

I can't log in to my account.

Please double-check that you are entering the correct email and password. If you have forgotten your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it. Also, make sure your keyboard's Caps Lock is not enabled.

My discount code is not working.

Please verify the discount code's terms and conditions. Some codes are for one-time use, have an expiration date, or are only applicable to specific products. Ensure you have entered the code correctly without any extra spaces.

Why can't I see my order history?

You must be logged into your account to view your order history. If you checked out as a guest, the order will not appear in an account's history. Please log in to see your past orders.
