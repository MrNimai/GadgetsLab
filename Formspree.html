ABOUT ME

WHO AM I?

Hi, I am Likhon Datta, a student and film enthusiast. Keenly interested in making fiction films, I also work in documentaries. My film journey started with working on a feature length documentary on Satyajit Ray – The World of Satyajit Ray (2021). This was my directorial debut. My second film is a feature fiction, shot entirely on a smartphone, called Eighteen to Twenty (2024). My third project is again another documentary, a feature length project on July Uprising of Bangladesh in 2024. This film, July Diaries (2026) is currently at editing stage and is expected to make its premiere on 01 July, 2026.
